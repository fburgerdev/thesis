\begin{mystatement}{definition}{suspension::rooted}[69] A \defterm{rooted microbundle} $\bb$ over $B$ is a microbundle over $B$ together with an isomorphism-germ \[ \germdef{R}{\bbb}{\be{b_0}}. \] Two rooted microbundles $\bb$ and $\bb'$ over $B$ are \defterm{rooted isomorphic} if there exists an isomorphism-germ $\germ{\bb}{\bb'}$ extending \[ \germdef{\inv{R'} \circ R}{\bbb}{\bb'\restr{b_0}}. \] \end{mystatement}

\begin{mystatement}{remark}{suspension::rooting} One can always define a rooting for a given microbundle by choosing a local trivialization in the base point and restricting it to the fiber of $b_0$. \end{mystatement}
