\definition{\parttitle{bundle-germ}} \\
Let $\bb$ and $\bb'$ be two microbundles over $B$ with the same fibre-dimension.
A map-germ
\[ F: (E, B) \double (E', B) \]
is a \defterm{bundle-germ} if a representative map $f: U \to E'$ maps each fibre $j^{-1}(b)$ bijective to a fibre $j'^{-1}(b')$.
